About Us

Cricket, Javelin, archery, and relay races are distinct athletic events featured in major competitions like the Olympics. Javelin involves throwing a spear-like implement as far as possible, requiring speed, strength, and technique. Archery involves shooting arrows at a target using a bow, demanding precision, control, and focus. Relay races involve teams of four runners each completing a segment of the race, passing a baton efficiently within a designated zone to achieve the fastest overall time.
